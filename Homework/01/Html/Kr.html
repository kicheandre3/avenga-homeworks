Krypton

Atomic number:

36

Atomic weight:

83.798

Krypton Kr is a noble gas with atomic number 36, discovered in 1898. It is a colorless, odorless, and tasteless gas at standard conditions, and is used in lighting, lasers, and imaging due to its unique spectral properties. Krypton is chemically unreactive, though it can form a few compounds with elements like fluorine, and exists as a gas in the Earth's atmosphere, making up about one part per million.
