Nitrogen

Atomic number:

7

Atomic weight:

14.0067

Nitrogen (N) is a nonmetallic element with atomic number 7, known for being the primary component of Earth's atmosphere (about 78%). It is a colorless, odorless, and tasteless gas that is essential for all living organisms, forming key parts of amino acids and nucleic acids. Its low reactivity as a diatomic gas (N2) is due to a strong triple bond, but it is a crucial element in industrial applications like fertilizer production, explosives, and the creation of inert environments.
