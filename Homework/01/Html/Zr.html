Zirconium

Atomic number:

40

Atomic weight:

91.224

Element 40 on the periodic table is zirconium (Zr), a shiny, silver-grey, corrosion-resistant transition metal that is in period 5, group 4. It has an atomic number of 40 and an atomic mass of approximately 91.22 amu. Zirconium is mainly used in nuclear reactors and in the chemical industry due to its high strength and resistance to heat and corrosion.
